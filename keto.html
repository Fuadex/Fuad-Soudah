Additional Information

ANX



U la la. This project was a tough nut to crack. In the crowd of groups of 3, I delivered a project alone in just merely 2 weeks. Why? Cause the previous ones crashed recurrently.

There is some beauty to such projects that are run in an extreme format. I had to redo everything: background research, design, prototyping and a final presentation which would last just 10 minutes, I packed in around 45 slides and delivered them almost impeccably on time. The products that I delivered... worked as prototypes would. The weightscale unfortunately would not detect weight due to defect load sensors and the application would run a series of mockups, emulating weight sensing due to the limited time I had to program all the features in.

In case of this project, I literally went in to hell and came back, damaged but happy with a positive outcome.
