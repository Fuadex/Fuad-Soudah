Influences

And the food in the city is quite different now that I think about it too. After all, I have one of the best kebab places in the area I was living in. Apparently, there is a coffee roastery just nearby too, which I sadly did not manage to visit during my brief stay. Shops are literally everywhere... What I keep finding Warsaw and Poland as a standalone to have an upperhand over other European even countries, is just the crazy amount of shops, with the scene so radically saturated that I find it difficult to comprehend how they still manage to create growth, keep opening new shops and just make money, cause there is just such a variety, all from convenience stores, towards discount stores, delicatessen, supermarkets, costco-alike and hypermarkets so even larger than the prior with new places being opened related to wholefoods and ecological and/or vegan stores of different sizes. In my area alone, in the radius of 5 km, I think you may find a fair double digit number of stores, coming closer towards triple digits. There are also more being opened and developed anyway but not that many bars or restaurants on the other hand which is weird. But most of these, you'll find in Warsaw with some chain stores that offer very high quality of foods for a very good price. Like Viking chain, where you just show up, choose what you want, grab a drink on your way out. A huge selection. Maccas got a bit worse recently but the fastfoods in this country are literally called restaurants and considering the foods they serve, they literally are restaurants, many of the franchises managed actually by the Polish. And then there are the smaller venues... and coffee places with chains being remarkably presentable. Somehow I'm seeing a shift towards serving quality and improving the quality of already offered merchandise, which is a very welcoming trends that I'm following in Poland. Even food at Ikea felt as if just better compared to other countries I've been visiting this chain in. It's still cheap but there's also a great selection span, if you're willing to grab a craftbeer, there will usually be quite a few to choose from. And some of the local produce that is just simply not available elsewhere than in Poland, primarily limited to particular fresh juices but not just.
