To think of it, there isn't much required or attached or anything, it's all pure imagination, the devil building all these fortresses of constraint. I'm quite good with coming up with entirely new ones. Popping the ones before with a simple touch, it's ridiculous how much you may achieve with as much but mine was of pure imagination. One that likes to revisit the old constructs.

I've been wondering for quite some time in regards as to whether I should actually start doing something. Take a look at the previous sentence. And once again? You see what I did there? I'm at a state in which I could go on forever with such, it's as if revisiting square one all over again. But the issue is yet more fundamental. Just, it's a different kind of fundamental. It's a fundament of inert and imagination. One that prompts me to revisit all the fortresses but more in relation to what prompted their construct. It's more of a way of combating my current state, as I know many other students get into the same flawed flow. All is there and in place but yet somehow the cogs just do not turn, all of a sudden. And I know why they are not turning. Except, that I also know what prompted the reason for why they are not turning. What exactly were the origins of it and only partially why I kind of succumbed to this inertness. At this point, I feel that this is an illness. And looking back I just wish that someone did something about that.

So instead of telling how fragile I've become all of a sudden and I will just move on and tell you how fragile I have become all of a sudden. Which is the epithome of what has become all of a sudden. This might be more of an illness symptom but does feel that something is off and I don't know exactly the reason why. Despite, that I feel like I am generally a very social fella and don't wish for anybody to feel bad or anything. Might it be that I can't use words properly? It all pains me to see because I know how productive I may become. I know how valuable I can be and how much good I can generate. All of which were qualities that I kept putting into question after years of a mixture consiting of work, neglect, anxiety and ambition. But what I managed to achieve throughout the years speak for itself. Even the works I felt stood negatively out, were not all that bad in the end. What I should be aiming for. What are the milestones that I should achieve. How I should prepare. In the end, after all these years I deducted, that University of Sydney, for all its glory, it provides opportunities. Many of which I have in scrutiny explored and maximised... but not quite.

It was clearly not working towards my wellbeing or personal growth. It made me a nobody and kept me at bay of not being able to achieve anything in life. Except, that I did see value in what I played. And I did see value in the tv shows I've watched. And I still managed to be with people to a limited amount of time I had. I... don't remember how I've been. I would have to look into the tons of photos and videos I have backed up from these years. It wasn't bad... But it wasn't also something I was looking towards achieving. My expectations greatly derailed with what I managed to scramble up. Turned out, the scramble was the optimum I could really get in my situation. But I never came to anywhere close where I aimed for...

But even if I did. I would have probably never ended up here. But if I did, I would not be necessarily in a better situation. And I would have never really became better prepared in the end.

And probably most of the other subjects altogether. Something was wrong throughout all these years and before but somehow I managed to top points at English. Which probably turned out to be yet another giggle elicited by the bi-polar luck I have tangled up with. I submited a portfolio, which could have boosted my chance of admission.
