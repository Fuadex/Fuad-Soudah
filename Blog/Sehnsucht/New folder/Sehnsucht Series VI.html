Olydorf

Propositions:

The largest issue encompassing Olympiadorf is the fact that newcomers actually need to make an effort in order to socialize and become part of the entire village as a whole. While it is true, that students have other chores from day to day such as primarily university itself and usually work in order to be able to make a living and afford, still extremely affordable, student housing, especially considering the qualities it delivers and provides the students with, makes the entire living there part something that seems even hard to believe once you try clarifying it to someone from the outside.

I propose the following:
- A tutor who would coordinate in conjunction with studentenwerk the integration of newcomers, whether exchange, domestic or international students.
- The tutor would get reimbursed similarly to today's 80 Euros for his/her work, but involving the newcomers to the village
- The integration would take place via the internet, through which evaluation would take place by the newcomers of the area. It would be a one-way evaluation, which would take each semester for the first 3 semesters
- There would be an utmost of 26 tutors in case of the bungalows. One per row.
- The tutors responsible for more people would be reimbursed higher compared to the others, the value would equal around 10 Euros per person, allocated to the tutor only in the case if the tutor manages to integrate the newcomer in the programs, that equals 3 Euros per semester per person who takes part in the programs. So: between 72 Euros to 168 Euros
- Bonuses for being able to integrate: 75%, 85%, 95% and 100% of the students, equal by an equivalent multiplier: 1,15, 1,3, 1,5 and 1,8.
- The allocated money will have to go: in 75% towards the dwellers, in 25% towards the tutor himself
- The tutors get elected via 3rd party means, that is a different system in which each vote directed is concealed (nobody knows who voted for whom)
- 12 Haussprecher located in the old bungalow buildings
- Responsible for matters that need to be mediated in between the students and the studentenwerk
- Needs to be within reach 24/7, each bungalow dweller gets assigned a personal one
- The Haussprecher will be evaluated by the end of each semester, based on their performance, they will receive an according amount of money: 3 Euros per person
- Bonus system: 75% - 1.15, 85% - 1.3%, 95% - 1.5, 100% - 1.8
- If the reputation drops below 65%, the housesprecher will crease to be one and a new election will have to take place
- On top of that, haussprecher will be allocated an additional amount of money equal to 1 Euro per person. The money will have to be directed towards revitalizing a certain part of the village, that could be a garden part, or a form of streetworkout. The idea will need to be communicated via electronic means and the minimum of 3 ideas will need to be introduced. Studentenwerk will be able to intervene in case if an idea seems way off
- The president and two vicepresidents may be elected only by the dwellers who have been in Olydorf for longer than 3 semesters in total. Their duty will be to represent the Olympic Village beyond it and spearhead the general direction of how the village will evolve. New developments will need to be discussed between Studentenwerk and the (vice)presidents and hence by them announced to the community. Haussprecher will need to report to the (vice)presidents the needs and adjustments in case of such projects. Furthermore, (vice)presidents will only be eligible to become such once they go through the entire system from bottom up, that is: tutor, haussprecher and then potentially (vice)presidents.
- The only way to take down (vice)presidents will be through a combination of votes, which will be allocated to: studentenwerk as an organisation, majority of haussprecher and majority of tutors. In case if a bid for repeal of (vice)presidents fails, they will still need to step down after the end of the term nevertheless. The maximum term for (vice)presidents will be just 2.
- The treasurer will be allocated by studentenwerk itself, who's responsibility will be to allocate the money to the tutors, haussprecher and (vice)presidents. Additionally, he/she will monitor the progress made each division of Olydorf. The duties will be strictly limited to just accounting, with an extra personal report view made confidentially to studentenwerk and (vice)presidents, who may publish it under circumstances in which potential unlawful decisions might have been undertaken
- The treasurer will have to be a student in Olydorf, who's specialization in which he/she will be majoring in will be related to accounting
- The (vice)presidents will receive in total of 1% of the total Olydorf's income, which will need to be directed at least at 90% towards ideas and works scheduled around Olydorf. The remaining 3%, 2% and 2% will go towards the (vice)presidents. The remaining 3% will be stored as collateral for any potential mishandling and 2% donated towards travel expenses in case of Olydorf's promotion

Critical Dillema

Ausschusses

- Each ausschuss will be evaluated by the community itself, gaining votes for either doing the job properly or not
- The participation in an ausschuss will lower the rent by 5 Euros and increase by 5 for each semester a student participates in. The deal does not multiply by participating in more than 1 ausschuss (so basically one person per one ausschuss)
- Participating in an ausschuss that is relevant according to the studies being undertaken by a student will automatically prolong their stay for every two semesters participated (and gained a total of minimum 65% of the community's satisfaction)
- Who becomes the head of the ausschuss is the solely matter of the ausschusss' themselves. The head will have to step down once the community's satisfaction drops under 65%. The stay at olydorf gets prolonged for each semester for the head of an ausschuss. Maximum term of 2 semesters, unless the satisfactory rate reaches 85% for the second semester and 95% for third. If the satisfactory rate keeps up at 95%, the head may stay as long as it stays equal or over 95%.

Introspekcja

Exchange Students

- Special evaluation directed towards them, who will be able to introduce their ideas and rate how each part of the village works. Their input may change the multiplier attached to haussprecher and tutors by: 1% if positive, -0.5% if none, -1.5% if negative. A satisfaction rate under 75% will result in the repeal of the according tutor, and under 70% will repeal the haussprecher allocated to the exchange students (in regards to exchange students)

Bungalows

- The rooftops will be now repurposed for the students to be used socially, with benches established, gardens and whatever is needed and decided by the rows of students.
- A garden will be established on the back of Olydorf

Mensa

- A Mensacafe will be established on top of the current Mensa, in which most employment possibilities will be offered for the students (the place will be run by studentenwerk, though). During the day, it will offer breakfasts and lunches. On evenings it will be a cafe available for everyone (lack of cheap and/or high volume alcohol).
