Technical Implementation

I made an attempt at showcasing how the concept scales, from within the animal's cognition, outwards towards the technology that influences, the environment it is embeded into and the entire ecosystem, which here is the Zoo.

The difficulty with situating the project in a Safari Zoo, is that there is heaps of land and therefore less likely that the animals will interact with, well, a structure that was previously unknown to them, unless it provides some additional qualities. For instance kangaroos, when it gets too hot, they will seek shelter and try to regulate their temeprature with saliva (which is the same mechanism for wallaroos and wallabies with the latter being in San Diego Zoo but San Diego not seeming to have scorching hot days).




Variations

I sketched 4 different related concepts-sketches:

- Virtual Reality for Humans with HTC Vive
- Hybrid Reality for Humans with a combo of 4 projectors
- Hybrid Reality for Humans with a combo of 4 projectors and 3D glasses
- Hybrid Reality for Animals with a combo of 4 projectors and props

All environments would run on a PC system with Unity

While it may seem out of relevance, I'd find it interesting to investigate how the interaction differs between Humans and Animals in relation to remotely the same hybrid environment.

Forth involves mixed reality environment for humans, concentrating rather on VR, although extending beyond and focusing on a few use cases
