Fleeting


I decided to make a change

How do you like it?

Thought I'd make the layout slightly cleaner, although honestly looking at the structure and looks of the website, I think it's high time to just simply raze it all to the ground. It doesn't end there, though. In all due thought process, I think the razing process should extend to other parts of my life and memories, although completing this process seems much more cumbersome than what the simplicity of razing seems to invoke.

The first weeks following my departure from my studies, at least for now called a leave of absense, were actually quite fruitful. I managed to backup lots of data, lose weight, do some stuff I kept postponing for years in fact. But as of pretty much last month, there was barely anything that I was doing, so in fact, I plunged into the well-known to me the state of, well, limbo, oblivion, nothingness, whatever one, you, whomever may call it. The state of stalemate.

I often think thought and as often I comeback to my earlier years and contemplate on many things. Random thoughts surface and memories, some crystal vivid as my life form and some fleeting as often I alight the train. I often think of writing all these as I think they're interesting tales to be told but I often withdraw to just simply thinking and knowing that most likely they won't surface at anytime anymore and life will continue for most with not much attention placed on fleeting things like life of my own.

It is a bit of a tough nut to crack, considering it's all a pandemic wraithing and many people not doing anything even or just doing what they can from home. Somewhat like myself in the very same bandwagon. Except that I am really doing anything and often even nothing and not much of a single thing starts to not bring any sense of joy to me, which starts to solidify into a problem.

The problem is as well, that I am running out of things to do. While I'm dieting, I have limited options as to what food I may eat. Spotify in my discover weekly began playing the same music for a while now, so it may seem it ran out of music to widen my own bubble. My father keeps playing the very same shows and music everyday. I'm reading pretty much the very same news, listening to the very next yet samey audiobooks, watching samey shows, movies and playing samey video games. They all are just starting to wash out and gray out, lacking much to offer just as myself.



One begins to wonder, as all of this continues to be fleeting, months after initially creating this entry. As the world continues to move forwards, I stay the same. Idle. Without an idea as for what I want to do in life anymore. Not really sure why I ended up in this place in the very beginning. Not sure what to do with any of my previous accomplishments as, at the time being shiny and exemplary, today they seem as brittle as a supermarket candy, so shiny at first, so craving at the time, content with how it tastes like, sort of leaving you wanting more and then wondering, what it was all for?

As I feel left alone by the surrounding world, one that I seem to be drifting further apart from, not being able to connect with and be content of the connection anymore, looking back and through the, fairly well documented lifetime as I had, covering the last 6 years of my life, there is lots of thoughts coming through my head, too many, vague and mouldy and all in haze of sorts.

And I'm also wondering whether any of this is actually worth sharing, to the outer world, as these are all dreams that can be presented to the outside world, dreams that some people can associate with, some of them, each and everyone, to some degree or perhaps none at all. Something you need to spend time to dig through. Some to contemplate or...

Perhaps look at the pretty pictures.

Just Pictures.

It's all that's really left after all.

Beautiful Dreams.
