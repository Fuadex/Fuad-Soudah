Introduction

Herzlich Wilkommen! My name is Fuad Soudah. I'm a 25 years old Creative Technologist.

My areas of expertise feature: Visual Effects, Interaction Design and Human-Computer Interaction. I synergically apply a wide range of knowledges acquired in my years dedicated to building my extensive skillset within the higher education sector, the programs in which I participated in took me through world's leading institutions based in the United States, Australia, Germany and South Korea.

I cultivate my love for an ever-increasing span of emotion-evoking media and dream of contributing to the real realms and fictional universes and a twine of both.

So, now you're probably wondering: 'What is this place?'

Well, it's my sandbox!
