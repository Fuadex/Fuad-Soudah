You end up at the Olympic Village in Munich for around 11 months, what do you do? I decided to join a few (almost all) committees and yet, Grasausschuss became my favorite as it enabled me to produce a lot of content, that is, specialize myself in certain ranges such as: graphic design, photography, documentation and language skills.

I co-drove Grasasusschuss's website as MMO (Mr. Media Outlet), producing captions for the picture I took of all the bungalows around. Aaand I also took some 360 videos, which were most sought after on the facebook's page. I'll stop now, cause my journey ain't finished yet.
