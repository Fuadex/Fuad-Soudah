Additional Information

At times I wrote about some other stuff. Basically different things, written insightful analysis, that could easily become research or essays, if tweaked. In fact, I already had the tendency to write about stuff in great lengths, making other peoples' heads spin, especially once I've seen some episodes of my favorite TV Shows. Last I remember, I could potentially have written over a 100 messages on facebook and the misfortunate fella would just complain and have a good laugh on what he woke himself up to.

Still, at times I took some serious stuff. For instance politics and, oh boy, how involved I got into reading in this case. At some point, I finally understood how dangerous getting passionate can actually be, as the ideas a person reads, have the potential to alter peoples' minds, especially the masses, once it's delivered the right way. Nevertheless, I was unhappy with the situation in Poland and after having read about its recent history, the contemporary, about the people who have connections with other people. Politicians, Media figures, the entire system, how it works and how corrupt in its form it is. Changes were coming and that I knew. The question remained, what kind of change would it be? And geopolitical elements came into play, I tried looking at the entire spectrum and... Well, today everything has changed. At the time, even facebook seemed like a platform carrying a great deal of wisdom. Not anymore. Yet, I did produce a few articles, just awaiting some alterations... And, if I may, I do truly believe, these articles, are in fact, insightful.

In Another World I tried writing and picturing what I learned from my stay in Jordan. There were so many layers that I captured during my stay there, which I could easily compare to other places, such as Poland, Australia. Nuances, that were truly one of a kind. Cultural differences, yet, not that dire. Just, what Jordan was intrigued me the most. In this case, there is not that huge material left, unfortunately. However, memories remain and so did the photos captured prevail.

There were other, minor series. They may become something larger than they are now. Who knows?
